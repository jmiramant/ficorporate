EverFi Celebrates

Financial Literacy Month

Every April, headlines across the US paint a bleak picture of America’s financial literacy. But here at EverFi, we have a whole lot of hope.

But here comes the hope part.

In the world of Twitter and Facebook, Millennials look to the Internet for everything from dinner recommendations to financial solutions. At EverFi, we use digital learning and gaming to empower students with the essentials to make smart financial decisions. In fact, 73% of students agree that interactive, online courses are an effective way for them to learn.

Join our mission.

EverFi has partnered with hundreds of banks, companies, and foundations across the US to bring new-media financial literacy education to over 5000 schools. And we can proudly say that 98% of our teachers would recommend this course to other educators. It’s time for the public and private sectors to work together to address this issue head on.
